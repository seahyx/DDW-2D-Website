What were our results?

An Interactive Visualisation

With our model trained and selected, we created an interactive visulaisation to see how various parameters impact each country differently. While the aim is to eliminate the prevalence of undernourishment, for some of these countries, the parameters applied here might not always be realistically possible, and potential solutions to complex social issues like solving undernourishment are often never as one dimensional as increasing a few parameters.

Parameter Variables

Consumer Price Index (CPI)

The Consumer Price Index (CPI) describes the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services. This can be used as a measure of inflation in food prices.

GDP per Capita

Gross domestic product per capita describes the standard measure of the value added created through the production of goods and services in a country. This can be used as a measure of the average income of citizens.
